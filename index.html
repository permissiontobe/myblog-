Our Mission 🌿

Hello, we are Fyth and Adam, the creators of Permission to Be. This place was born out of quiet rebellion. A rebellion against performance and labels. Against the exhausting need to be healed, enlightened, good, impressive, in order to be loved, to arrive or achieve your goals.

Here, right and wrong loosen their authority. Where no version of you needs to be edited for approval. Where your doubt is allowed, your ambition is allowed, your softness is allowed, your rage is allowed, your contradictions are allowed, your fear is allowed, and your procrastination is allowed. Even the parts of you that feel like the villain in someone else’s story — especially those. Here, in fact, all these labels drop.

Permission to Be is about sitting with yourself long enough to hear your own voice beneath expectations, beneath fear, beneath inherited scripts.

Bring all of you — the polished and the messy. The spiritual and the skeptical. The gentle and the wild. The lost and the seeker. No performance is required, only honesty.

We created this space because we know what it feels like to believe you have to be someone for love to be earned, for success to be achieved, for worth to be earned.

So this blog is our decision to unlearn that. It is where we practice existence as is. It is where we bring the whole of us and embrace those parts. It is where we allow ourselves to question, to evolve, to contradict ourselves, to take up space and most of all, to be.

If you are learning to do the same, you are not walking alone.

This is your permission. And ours. To be.

— Fyth & Adam
